How We Met

24.02.2020

Stella and Harry first crossed paths at the McDonald's in Batemans Bay, where Stella was a manager at the time completing Harry's orientation. After Harry completed his shift, he noticed Stella returning to the store. Harry attempted to hide, as she gave him butterflies. As fate would have it, they struck up a conversation and instantly connected. The following day, they arranged a few dates, and from that moment on, there was no turning back.

The Proposal

21.12.2023

During a holiday visit to spend Christmas with Harry's family, one afternoon found Stella accompanying him and his parents to the top of a hill behind their house, just as the sun began its descent. Captivated by the scenic beauty, they seized the moment to capture memories with a few photographs. As the golden hues of sunset bathed the landscape, Harry, with a heart full of love and anticipation, invited Stella to dance. With grace, he spun her around, and as she turned back to him, she found him on bended knee, ready to embark on a new chapter of their lives together.

We are so excited to celebrate this next chapter of our lives with you all.
